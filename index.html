About Me

Hi, I'm Isobel and I'm interested in using child-robot interaction for social good. I'm a 4th year PhD student at The University of Edinburgh and Heriot-Watt University, supervised by Dr Michael Herrmann, Prof. Lynne Baillie, and Prof. Joanne Williams. My PhD is on developing a robot for animal welfare education.

Before starting my PhD, I completed my BA and MEng in Engineering at Trinity College, Cambridge, with an exchange year spent studying at the National University of Singapore. My master’s project was at the Bio-Inspired Robotics Laboratory under the supervision of Dr Fumiya Iida on autonomous dishwasher loading from cluttered canteen trays. Outside my PhD, I enjoy swimming, cooking, and solving cryptic crosswords.
