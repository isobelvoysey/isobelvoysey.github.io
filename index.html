About Me

Hi, I'm Isobel and I'm interested in using child-robot interaction for social good. I'm a 4th year PhD student at The University of Edinburgh and Heriot-Watt University, supervised by Dr Michael Herrmann, Prof. Lynne Baillie, and Prof. Joanne Williams. My PhD is on developing a robot for animal welfare education.
