The Brilliant Club Scholars Programme

Over the past two years I've worked as a PhD tutor on the Brilliant Club's Scholars Programme. This programme links PhD researchers with state schools to run a short course on a topic ties to their research. I've designed my own course on social robots including topics like anthropomorphism, the uncanny valley, the baby schema, and non-verbal communication. Taught this course across 3 different schools to 41 teenagers.
