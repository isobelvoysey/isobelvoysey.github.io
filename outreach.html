Outreach Activities

The Brilliant Club Scholars Programme

Over the past two years I've worked as a PhD tutor on the Brilliant Club's Scholars Programme. This programme links PhD researchers with non-selective state schools to run a seven-week course on a topic tied to their research, giving students a chance to experience university-style learning and build skills for higher education. I've designed my own course on social robots including topics like anthropomorphism, the uncanny valley, the baby schema, and non-verbal communication, and taught it to 41 teenagers across 3 different schools.

The University of Edinburgh Informatics Circle

I've helped organise and run online computer science activities for 7--14-year-olds, including developing a new activity to teach the basics of digital logic.
